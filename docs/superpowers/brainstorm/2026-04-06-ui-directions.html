B. Gallery Rail

Cleaner and more efficient. Still premium, but less theatrical. Best if you want to reduce scrolling and see more content per screen.

  • Smaller hero footprint with denser content rails underneath
  • Navigation and filters become sharper and quieter
  • Details screens prioritize clarity over spectacle
  • Safer redesign, but less distinctive than Direction A

C. Editorial Spotlight

More like a prestige magazine layout. Bigger type, stronger narrative sections, and more text-forward detail pages.

  • Meta Details becomes the star with editorial sections and cast/crew blocks
  • Browse pages feel more curated than app-like
  • Great for premium storytelling, weaker for quick scanning
  • Higher design risk and a bigger implementation delta
Recommendation: A. Cinema Stage. It matches your Apple TV reference best, gives the ratings area room to feel important, and fixes the current mismatch between the strong hero and weaker downstream metadata/carousel presentation.
